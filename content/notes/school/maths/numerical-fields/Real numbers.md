- $\mathbb{R}\in\mathbb{Q}\cup\mathbb{I}$
- $a;b;c;d\in\mathbb{R}$:
    - IF($a>b$ AND $b>c$) THEN($a>c$)
    - IF($a>b$ AND $c>0$) THEN($a\cdot c>b\cdot c$)
    - IF($a>b$ AND $c<0$) THEN($a\cdot c<b\cdot c$)
    - IF($a>b$ AND $c\in\mathbb{R}$) THEN ($a+b>b+c$)
    - IF($a>b$ AND $c>d$) THEN($a+c>b+d$)
